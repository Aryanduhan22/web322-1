Peggy's Cove is a small rural community located on the eastern shore of St. Margaret's Bay in Nova Scotia, Canada. It's famous for its iconic lighthouse and the unique, granite formations that surround it. Peggy's Cove is a popular tourist destination and attracts visitors from around the world due to its picturesque scenery and rich maritime history. The area is also known for its strong connection to the ocean, and offers opportunities for fishing, boating, and other ocean-related activities.
